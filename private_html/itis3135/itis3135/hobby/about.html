I love mountain biking and it is my favorite hobby of all time ever. I have been into the sport since I was 9 years old. To put that into context, I am now 21 years old. Check out my page to learn more about my mountain biking journey and mountain biking in general!

I currently ride a Trek Roscoe 7. It has a SRAM NX drivetrain, RockShox fork as the suspension, and is built on a light-weight aluminum frame. It is a hardtail which means that it has suspension in the front and does not have any in that back.
