Website Evaluations

Good Website

Apple made a fantastic webpage for the MacBook Pro products, nicely detailing their key features with cool graphics.

  • Contrast: Switches between light and dark on the site. It incorporates the use of great contrast.
  • Repetition: There is little to no repetition with words describing the features of the products. There is a good amount of repetition of content colors and backgrounds which helps with consistency.
  • Alignment: Everything is formatted properly and is organized
  • Proximity: Big pictures gives better understanding for user about what they are supposed to be looking at.

Bad Website

Berkshire Hathaway is a company that indulges in many different industries such as insurance, manufacturing, energy, rail, and retail owned by billionaire Warren Buffett. This website is as bare bones as it gets as it only has links to their businesses.

  • Contrast: The whole page is white with purple links to Berkshire Hathaway's businesses.
  • Repetition: There is a type of consistency but not in a positive way. The site is too bland so that the style remains the same with no change throughout.
  • Alignment: The main content of the site is centered whereas the last two thirds of the footer is way too the left and not formatted correctly.
  • Proximity: There are not any examples of proximity as everything on this webpage blends in. All of the links are formatted the same and one could not tell the difference between them.
